Patient Resources & FAQ

Yes. Diagnostic imaging exams are generally performed upon the request of a physician or nurse practitioner. You must have a valid requisition form signed by your healthcare provider. You can upload this during our online booking process.

Preparation depends on the exam type. For abdominal exams, typically fast for 6-8 hours. For pelvic exams, a full bladder is usually required (drink 1L of water 1 hour prior). Specific instructions will be given upon booking confirmation.

We prioritize booked appointments to minimize wait times. We do accommodate urgent walk-in X-ray requisitions whenever capacity allows, but we highly recommend booking online to ensure timely service.
